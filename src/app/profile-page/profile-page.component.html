Our Vision:

We want to change present scenario of Agriculture, So we want to serve innovative agriculture information so we will provide all type of information at anytime at anyplace free of cost

Our Mission:

Encourage peoples to information related to agriculture at one spot i.e. My Agriculture Information Bank.

Our Objectives:

1. To promote the practicing farmers for Organic Farming.

2. Encourage peoples to read about agriculture science by provide free online quality agriculture information.

3. To create the one spot agriculture information center.

4. Provide readymade study materials to the agriculture students, etc…

5. Develop: We will develop the condition of Organic Farming in India with the help of our educated peoples.

6. Empower: Ask to peoples to empower farmers to go for new innovative techniques.

7. To provide free information to the agriculture professionals at anywhere and at anytime whenever they required.

8. Educate: Educate the peoples related to the theory & practical of agriculture by creating one spot innovative information center for organic farming.
